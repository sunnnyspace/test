🇺🇸 English Tourist Interview Script

Interview #1
🎬 Opening
Hi there! We're doing a quick survey about foreign tourists' experiences in Korea.
Do you have about 3 minutes for a few simple questions?
한국 여행 경험 조사 중이에요. 3분만 시간 내주실 수 있어요?
💬 Casual version
Sorry to bother you! We're doing a short survey — just 3 minutes, would that be okay?
❓ Core Questions (9)
1
Background
Is this your first visit to Korea? How many times have you been?
한국 방문이 몇 번째예요?
📌 Expected answers
First time! / Second visit / Been a few times now
2
TranslationKEY ⭐
Do you use any translation apps while traveling? Which one?
번역 앱 써요? 어떤 거?
📌 Expected answers
Google Translate / Papago / DeepL / Naver / Don't really use one
3
TranslationKEY ⭐
Have you heard of or used Papago? What did you think?
Papago 써보셨어요? 어떠셨어요?
📌 Expected answers
Yes, it's great! / Never heard of it / Prefer Google Translate
4
TranslationKEY ⭐
When do you find translation apps most useful?
번역 앱 어떤 상황에서 가장 필요해요?
💬 Follow-up
Like ordering food? Shopping? Reading signs? Getting around?
📌 Expected answers
Ordering at restaurants / Shopping / Reading menus / Signs on the subway
5
Navigation
Have you had any trouble navigating with map apps?
지도 앱 쓸 때 어려움 있었어요?
💬 Follow-up
Like Google Maps not working properly? Or not knowing which app to use?
📌 Expected answers
Google Maps was fine / Subway was confusing / Street addresses were hard to follow
6
Payment
How do you usually pay when shopping? Card? Apple Pay? Cash?
쇼핑할 때 결제를 주로 어떻게 해요?
📌 Expected answers
Credit/debit card / Apple Pay / Google Pay / Cash / Some places didn't take my card
7
Payment
Did the tax refund process work out smoothly for you?
면세 환급 과정 순조로웠어요?
💬 Follow-up
Any issues? Did you know how the refund kiosks work?
📌 Expected answers
Pretty easy, actually / Didn't know about it / The kiosk was confusing / Didn't bother
8
Info Sources
Where did you get your travel info about Korea?
한국 여행 정보 어디서 찾으셨어요?
💬 Follow-up
Instagram? YouTube? Reddit? TikTok? Travel blogs?
📌 Expected answers
YouTube travel vlogs / Instagram / Reddit (r/korea) / TikTok / Lonely Planet / Friend's recommendation
9
Open-endedKEY ⭐
What's been the most inconvenient thing about traveling in Korea?
한국 여행 중 뭐가 가장 불편했어요?
📌 Expected answers
Language barrier / Confusing subway / Some places cash-only / Hard to find English menus / WiFi issues
🎬 Closing
That's everything! Thank you so much for your time. 😊
Hope you enjoy the rest of your trip in Korea!
참여해주셔서 정말 감사합니다! 한국 여행 즐겁게 하세요!
🎁 With a gift
We have a small gift to say thank you for participating!

No worries at all! Enjoy your trip! 😊
